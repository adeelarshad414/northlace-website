The Cloud Lounge is the third, more conversational format alongside Adeel Codes
Cloud and Signal & Scale: the same expertise, with more relaxed delivery.
Cloud, DevOps, site reliability engineering (SRE), platform work, and now
artificial intelligence (AI) are talked through like you are not on call.

It is hosted by Adeel Arshad, Head of DevOps & Cloud at Tkxel and Cloud Native
Computing Foundation (CNCF) Kubestronaut. If Adeel Codes Cloud is the build
bench and Signal & Scale is the filter, The Cloud Lounge is the room where the
conversation can breathe.
