Northlace now publishes its brand systems as a working part of the site, not as
a folder of loose files.

The <a href="/brand">brand systems hub</a> links to the four current systems:
Northlace, Adeel Codes Cloud, Signal & Scale, and The Cloud Lounge. Each page
includes the logo story, color system, typography rules, voice, pillars,
do/don't guidance, and manifest-backed downloads.

The kits include SVG logo variants, a brand-guidelines PDF, a PPTX deck
template, and an Open Graph image for each brand. They are generated from
`src/data/brands.ts` so the website, downloads, and manifest stay in sync.

Some destinations are still intentionally marked as to be announced. Social
links, final domains, The Cloud Lounge naming checks, and Northlace trademark
clearance should be confirmed before they become public claims.
